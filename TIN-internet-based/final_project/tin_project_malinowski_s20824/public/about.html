What is SHARCit?

SHARCit simplifies sharing music for artist and finding that music for users.

It allows:

Who? To do what? How?
Artist to share the music by providing link to the song OR the file itself
User to find the desirable release by browsing through the SHARCit catalog
Everyone to connect with each other by providing connection stream
